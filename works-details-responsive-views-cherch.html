Responsive Views for Church Website

Visit online

Design a responsive website for local church organizations which need to raise money to support families or individuals in their local community.

Conducting interviews, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, and iterating on designs.

The Problem
Not friendly website for non-tech-savvy users

The goal
Create less clutter website easier to navigate. Improve accessibility.

User research summary
I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to understand the users needs. A primary user group identified through research was people that are the members of the cherch ages 40-70, who are non-tech-savvy.

Target users
People that are the members of the cherch ages 40-70, who are non-tech-savvy.

Role
Web Design, UX / UI
